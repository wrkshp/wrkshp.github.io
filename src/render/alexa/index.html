Setup

Before coming into the workshop, you'll need...

  • An Amazon account. Visit amazon.com and create an one, no credit card necessary.
  • An AWS account linked to your Amazon account. Visit aws.amazon.com to do so.
  • At developer.amazon.com, you'll create an Amazon developer account and link it to your Amazon account.
  • A text editor. We recommend Sublime or VIM, but any text editor works!
