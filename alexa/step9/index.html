Launching

The final step of your skill would be to publish your skill into the Alexa Skills Store. In any other skill, you will move onto the Launch tab at the top of the page and complete the fields as it asks. These are fairly self explanatory, and you should be able to figure them out yourselves. Unfortunately, you will not be able to publish this particular Pokemon skill, as Pokemon is copyrighted by Nintendo.

If it's any incentive, Amazon offers a free shirt each month to developers who successfully publish a skill. Make sure to follow all the terms and conditions in order to get the shirt. And, if your skill becomes one of the top engaging skills, Amazon will give you money.

Other than that...

You did it!
